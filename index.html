Phases

1. Online Elimination Round


February 12 - February 23, 2025

  • Teams can choose from provided problem statements or submit their own, provided it is moderate and aligns with the societal and moral impact theme.
  • Self-proposed problem statements will go through a screening process.
  • Teams will be notified via email if rejected within 2 days of registration. If no email is received, the statement is considered accepted.
  • Teams must submit an idea presentation (PPT). No working prototype is required.
  • Results Announcement: February 25, 2025.

2. Offline Hackathon


February 27-28, 2025

  • The top 40 teams from Phase 1 will compete.
  • Teams must build a prototype from scratch at the venue.
  • Use of prebuilt code will result in disqualification.
  • AI tools are allowed, but prebuilt solutions are prohibited.
  • Prototype Requirement: 40-50% working prototype.
